I'm Nate — I grew up in the **Bay Area** and I'm studying **applied math and economics
at Vanderbilt**, with minors in data science and computer science.

I build things that take live data and turn it into a clear answer. SurfScore reconciles
messy buoy reports against forecast data to score the surf. dailybriefmail searches the
web every morning and writes one short brief per reader. This site works the same way:
the terrain is real USGS elevation data, and the ocean is driven by a live NOAA buoy off
the California coast right now — and it tells you plainly when that buoy goes quiet.

I surf, which is how the forecasting work started.
