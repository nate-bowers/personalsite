I grew up in the **Bay Area** and now study **applied math and economics at Vanderbilt**.
The throughline is using data to answer questions I actually care about — markets,
oceans, and the systems underneath both.

I like building things that are real on the first day: tools that read live data,
make a decision, and tell you something true. The site you're on is one of them — the
ocean behind this panel is driven by a NOAA buoy reporting off the California coast
right now.

Outside the terminal: surfing when the swell shows up, and watching how a coastline
behaves over a season.
