I'm Nate. I grew up in the **Bay Area** and I'm a junior at Vanderbilt studying **applied
math and economics**, with minors in data science and computer science. I've always liked
numbers, and applied math and economics is where they meet real problems: real systems,
real decisions, real stakes. That's the kind of problem I like to work on.

I also like starting things and figuring them out as I go. In 2023 I co-founded a
window-cleaning business, Clean View Crew, in Atherton, California. I ran the sales door to
door, managed a crew of ten, and we cleared $60K+ in revenue across 100+ clients. Now I
mostly build software: tools that take live data and turn it into a decision. Different
thing, same instinct: start scrappy and ship something that works.

Outside of that, I'm usually outdoors: surfing, golf, camping, national parks, and skiing
since I was four. The rest of the time it's electronic music and Formula 1 (I pull for
McLaren). The coast on this page is home.
